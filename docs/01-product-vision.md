# Product Vision

School OS is an offline-first, desktop-first school management platform built for low-connectivity environments in Ghana and similar markets.

## Mission
Enable schools to run admissions, academics, finance, and reporting reliably even when internet and power are unstable.

## Principles
- Offline-first by default
- Desktop-first operations (Windows first)
- Safe sync when internet returns
- Modular architecture for long-term SaaS growth
